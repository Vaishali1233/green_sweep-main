GreenSweep

GreenSweep optimises government cleaning services and addresses community concerns to revolutionise urban trash management. Through the platform's user-friendly interface, users may upload images to report issues such as overflowing dustbins, enabling them to take an active role in contributing to cleaner environments. In order to effectively handle identified high waste locations, GreenSweep simultaneously gives government cleaning services real-time data and optimised routes.
